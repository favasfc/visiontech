Message From The Founder

“SINCE MORE THAN 27 YEARS AGO,MEMCO ENGINEERING CO. HAS STARTED FROM HUMBLE BEGINNINGS TO EMERGED INTO ONE OF THE ESTABLISHED FIRMS IN THE UAE IN PROVIDING ENGINEERING WORK TO SEVERAL DIFFERENT PLAYERS IN THE CONSTRUCTION INDUSTRY.

WE BELIEVE THAT TO BE AHEAD OF THE PACK, WE NEED TO ANTICIPATE THE MARKET NEEDS, WORK CLOSELY WITH CLIENTS AND ITS REPRESENTATIVES AND COORDINATE HONESTLY WITH SUPPLIERS. THIS IS DESPITE THE FACT THAT DURING THE YEAR 2009, MARKED BY THE GLOBAL FINANCIAL CRISIS, MAJORITY OF BUSINESSES HAS CLOSED DOWN ITS OPERATIONS AND YET WE CONTINUE TO BE ON OUR FEET THANKS TO THE SUPPORT OF SERVICES OFFERED BY THE GOVERNMENT OF UNITED ARAB EMIRATES AND ITS FAIR LAWS. WE HAD PROVEN THAT WE CAN DO SO BY BEING TRANSPARENT IN OUR DEALINGS WITH ALL THE CLIENTS, SUPPLIERS AND TO ITS OWN WORKING STAFF.

TODAY, AS WE HAVE GREAT OPPORTUNITIES IN THE COMING YEARS, memco LOOKS FORWARD TO TAKING ON NEW CHALLENGES IN THE FUTURE WITH THE CONTINUED SUPPORT OF OUR CUSTOMERS AND INTERNAL WORK FORCE.

LAST BUT NOT LEAST, WE ARE EXTREMELY GRATEFUL TO THE UAE GOVERNMENT FOR ENABLING OUR CONTINUED SUCCESS AND PROSPERITY IN THE BUSINESS”


ISAM AL SHARABATI
Founder / Managing Director
